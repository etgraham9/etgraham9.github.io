Choosing the Right Wood

Wood on the side of the road is great to practice with You can build all kinds of cool stuff without having to worry about the cost. Just make sure it's not rotten!

My Policy on Mistakes

Mistakes are fine and add to the charm. You know what they say, "Measure twice, cut three or four times after you mess up".
