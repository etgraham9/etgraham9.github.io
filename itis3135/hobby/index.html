Why Does the Reading Group Exist?

When we created this reading group we wanted a place for people who love books to be able to talk. Here's why we think it's important:

  • Reading helps build a bigger vocabulary.
  • Finding new books can be tricky and getting recommendations can be a great way to expand your horizons.
  • Having someone to talk about a book with can help you engage in a more meaningful way.
